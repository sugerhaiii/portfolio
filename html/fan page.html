The Marten

The diet of this little creature is very varied, for the marten it eats everything it can catch!

But if not they eat red-backed voles, deer mice, hares, grouse, squirrels, etc.

The mammal also eats amphibians and bird eggs.

We can not forget that he also eats berries, especially raspberries and blueberries.

The marten lives in mixed forests or sometimes mountains in North America.

They lives from 10 to 12 years.
